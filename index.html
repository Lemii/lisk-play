Frequently Asked Questions

Go Back

How does this thing work?

The goal of this contest is to predict the price of Bitcoin on (opening price of the day). The prediction closest to the price wins.


How do I submit my prediction?

To submit a prediction, simply move the slider to your desired amount (or enter a value manually), and click 'Submit'.

If you have a Lisk wallet installed (eg: Lisk Hub, Lisk Nano, Liskish Wallet), it will automatically open with the recipient, reference and amount fields pre-populated.


Can I manually submit a prediction without using the website?

Yes, you can. Please use the following info:

Recipient:
Reference: Your prediction (examples: $6510, $2400, $3000, etc. Other formats are not allowed)
Amount: LSK

One prediction per account!


What happens if I submit multiple predictions from one account?

The prediction that was first submitted will count.


What happens if I submit a prediction that was already placed by someone else?

The prediction that was first submitted will count.

(This can only happen when you manually submit a prediction, since the form on the website validates each submission)


What happens if I submit a prediction with a different LSK amount?

Submissions placed with less than 1 LSK will be considered invalid. However, the LSK amount that is sent will be added to the jackpot.

Submissions placed with more than 1 LSK will behave as regular predictions. The excess LSK will also be added to the jackpot.

(This can only happen when you manually submit a prediction, since the form on the website automatically configures the correct amount)


Why does the size of the jackpot mutate?

The jackpot increases with each prediction that is placed. On top of that, additional bonusses may be active (provided by sponsors).


Are you making money off this?

No, Lisk Play! is a community effort.


Are you affiliated to LiskHQ?

No, Lisk Play! is in no way or form affiliated with LiskHQ or Lightcurve. See the full disclaimer for more info.
